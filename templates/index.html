Welcome to DivideNDust

Where chores meet red tape!

DivideNDust can help you easily divide the cleaning tasks in your home and keep everyone accountable that the work gets done. Easily add your tasks, invite your house colleagues and generate a 30 days cleaning schedule. Log in daily to mark your tasks as complete. Win points every time you complete a task and use those points creatively (mainly pass your tasks to someone else). Cleaning is never fun, but having an app about it, could be.

How it works?

Assuming it does...
1.

First, login. If you have received an e-mail invitaiton, you should already be part of a house and maybe even have a task assigned. If not, you have the option to become a House Master and create a cleaning schedule yourself.

2.

Start adding tasks that need done and invite your flat mates. You assign each task a difficulty, from 1 to 20 and based on this, the "algorithm" divides all that tasks between flat mates. You can create a cleaning schedule for the next 30 days.

3.

Login daily to mark your tasks as complete and win points that you can then use to pass these cleaning tasks to someone else, skip them or just procrastinate (as usual) and then feel bad about yourself.

It used to take us 8, 9 hours to get our apartment clean. But now, with the admin work, and the bugs and the database errors, we are already up at 12 hours. What can I say? Amazing app.
