Welcome to Clean Divide

Clean Divide can help you easily divide the cleaning tasks in your apartment and keep everyone accountable that the work gets done. Be warned though, it's still you that needs to do the cleaning, I'm just doing the app.

Continue with Google

How it works?

Assuming it does...
1.
Login here, using your Google account. To save all your tasks, flatmates and rooms, we need users to be logged in.
2.
Start adding rooms you need cleaned, flatmates and tasks. Every task is worth a certain number of points (add the points based strictly on the difficulty, not on the frequency).
3.
When you click "Generate", the algorith divides all the tasks equally between participants and sends your flatmates an invitation. Once they join they can see exactly what needs to be done today, tomorrow, etc.
